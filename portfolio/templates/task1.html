Coleslaw Sandwich Mug Cake Dalgona Coffee
Healthy eating is a big no amongst the kids today and the simplest reason they can give for it is obviously it is not tasty. Coleslaw sandwich is an easy, healthy option when you feel lazy and yet in a mood for something tasty and healthy. Dive your spoon into this sweet Honey Mug Cake. A soft, sticky and gooey mug cake made with honey, then topped with a delicious buttercream frosting. A delicious cake in less than 5 minutes! Mug cakes are basically an amazing dessert that can be made quickly and are a quick delight to all the cake lovers. Nothing, not even the pandemic could keep one away from enjoying a cup of coffee. So, during the quarantine, chill out, surfing the internet with a cup of dalgona coffee the way you like it. It is nothing but a cappuccino turned on its head, with the frothy coffee on top and the milk underneath.
